  2. Then this remains, that we divide our power.

  4. Towards York shall bend you with your dearest speed,
  5. To meet Northumberland and the prelate Scroop,
  6. Who, as we hear, are busily in arms:
  7. Myself and you, son Harry, will towards Wales,
  8. To fight with Glendower and the Earl of March.
